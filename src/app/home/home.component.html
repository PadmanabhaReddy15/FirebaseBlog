Phoenix Drone

This project was started in the year 2015 with a group of three. Designed from basic knowledge about drones, and implementation of existing drone technology. Research is being done for further improvement of this model in most efficient and reliable way. As drone technology is very complex this site is created to share knowledge, views, ideas about drones in a simpler way to some extent. Main objectives start from basics of Drones, flight parameters, mathematical modelling, communication model, technologies involved in designing them etc.
